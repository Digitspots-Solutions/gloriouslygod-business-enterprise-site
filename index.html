About Us

GLORIOUSLYGOD BUSINESS ENTERPRISE is a trusted name in Etsako West, combining modern financial convenience with agricultural excellence. Located along Ughiole Power line in Aviele, Edo State, we serve our community by providing essential POS banking services and supplying fresh, quality farm produce. Our dual focus allows us to meet the everyday needs of individuals, families, and businesses across the region. We pride ourselves on reliability, integrity, and our commitment to supporting the economic growth of our community.

Our Services

POS Services

We provide fast, secure, and reliable Point of Sale transactions for all your banking needs. Cash withdrawals, deposits, transfers, and bill payments - all at competitive rates with minimal waiting time.

Farm Produce Supply

Fresh, quality agricultural products directly from local farms. We supply yams, cassava, vegetables, grains, and seasonal produce to households, restaurants, and retailers. Bulk orders welcome.

Cash-In/Cash-Out

Convenient cash services for mobile money and bank transactions, serving customers across Etsako West and surrounding areas.

Agricultural Consultation

Guidance on farm produce quality, seasonal availability, and bulk procurement for commercial buyers.
